Interests

Personal Interests

  • Music: I've been playing guitar since I was 12 years old, and I'm currently working through an adult's beginner guide to piano. I've tinkered in composition, both acoustic and electronic.
  • Writing: I write short stories and novel-length fiction. I have completed one novel draft and have several others in varying stages of progress. When offered, I often take creative writing courses through The Writer's Studio at Simon Fraser University.
  • Travelling: I am particularly fond of 4wd camping voyages through the Australian Outback...alone. I've been lucky enough to have done this three times so far, and blogged about it in 2012, 2014, and 2017.

Recent Projects

  • I've started drafting notes for a new novel or novella that combines ideas behind alternative timelines and reincarnation/resurrection. Yes, it will probably end up as convoluted an idea as it sounds...
