About Me

I have been a software engineer in the video games industry for almost 20 years, spanning four generations of consoles and several shipped titles. The majority of my time in the industry has been spent working in the audio domain, though I also have some experience in rendering, engine systems, and gameplay.

I am passionate about the art of video game sound! I enjoy working with audio artists, content professionals, and fellow programmers to help provide a voice to the game, as well as building and streamlining tools and workflows so artists can spend more of their time doing what they do best - making the best game audio possible!

Some Cool Accomplishments

  • Shipped the first EA Sports game with the custom user music feature that allowed the player to replace any or all of the game soundtrack with their own personal music.
  • Worked on the interactive music feature found in SSX (2012) that dynamically altered the soundtrack based on player input and gameplay.
  • Extensive credits list includes FIFA, NHL, SSX, UFC, Madden, Age of Empires 4, Company of Heroes 3, as well as several additional credits in other EA published titles.
